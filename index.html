Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unused items in their original packaging. Please contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days, while express shipping takes 1-2 business days. International shipping may take 7-14 business days.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination.

Are your products covered by warranty?

All our products come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this to track your package on our website or the carrier's website.
